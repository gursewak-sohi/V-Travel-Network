About Us

V Travel Network is a niche wholesaler providing representation in Australia and New Zealand, for International Operators providing services for the Independent (and sometimes not so Independent) Traveller.

We proudly represent Bamba Experience (World) , Moose Travel (Canada) and The Dragon Trip (China & Japan), Fez Travel (Turkey) just to name a few.

V Travel Network was founded 8 years ago by a group of avid travellers and our team has worked in nearly all aspects of Tourism and Hospitality in over 30 countries around the world Throughout their various roles and travels they realised that the most memorable experiences were the ones that got them off the standard tourist trail and allowed them to experience the destination at their own pace.

Throughout their various roles, they developed a strong connection with the various Independent Travel Networks around the Globe, which in turn has driven the desire to maximise awareness of this unique style of travel, that allows the benefits of Independent Travel to be enjoyed by all types of travellers. Whether they be heading out on their first overseas trip, or one of those more experienced travellers that just no longer have the time to travel at the pace they once did, but still want to see and do as much as possible along the way.

Over the last few years, V Travel Network has been fortunate to welcome a range of new members to our team, that also have extensive experience in all aspects of the Travel Industry (and quite a few others) and proudly excel in their commitment to customer service at all levels to ensure every traveller has the best experience possible.

V Travel Network is proudly an ATAS Accredited Travel Wholesaler (A10862) and a proud member of both ATTA, AFTA, IATA and CATO (TO1117)
